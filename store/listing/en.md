## Description
Truly free — no watermark, no time limit, no subscription, no ads. Record,
edit frame by frame, and export best-in-class GIFs with one lightweight app
(about 15 MB). No account, no sign-in, no internet required.

You record with a capture frame: a movable, resizable window that shows exactly
what will be recorded. It remembers its size and position so your next capture
starts instantly, and a single checkbox switches to full-screen. Capture is
smooth at up to 60fps.

Stop recording and every frame lands in the editor. Delete frames, adjust
timing and playback speed, crop, resize, and undo anything. One click trims
the motionless moments at the start and end, and merging duplicate frames can
shrink your file dramatically.

Messed up one part? Don't re-record everything. Continue recording captures
just that part and inserts it where you want — at the start, after the current
frame, or at the end. Each new take is auto-grouped as a colored block you can
move as a unit. That's VoidGif's multi-take editing.

Export is where VoidGif shines. GIFs are encoded with the gifski engine —
thousands of colors per frame, no banding. Lossless APNG, PNG sequences, and
H.264 MP4 are built in too. See the estimated file size before you export,
or pick a GitHub / Discord / Slack preset and VoidGif fits your GIF under the
limit automatically. When it's done, copy it straight to the clipboard and
paste it anywhere.

Your work is auto-saved: if the app ever closes unexpectedly, you can recover
on the next launch. And everything happens on your device — VoidGif makes no
network connections and collects no data.

Key features
- Region or full-screen capture with a size/position-remembering frame, up to 60fps
- Multi-take editing: continue recording, choose the insert position, move takes as colored blocks
- Frame editing: delete, duplicate, reorder, per-frame delay, crop, resize, undo/redo
- Open existing GIFs to edit and re-export — trim, retime, crop, and shrink them
- One-click trim of static lead-in/lead-out, duplicate-frame merging
- Playback speed control (all/selection/per-take), loop finishing (ping-pong, seam preview)
- File size estimate before export + GitHub/Discord/Slack/X size presets with auto-fit
- gifski-powered high-quality GIF, lossless APNG, PNG sequence, H.264 MP4
- Copy to clipboard right after export
- Auto-save with crash recovery
- Dark & light themes, English · 한국어 · 日本語
- Global hotkeys: F7 start/pause, F8 stop
- Fully offline. No account, no ads, no tracking, no data collection
